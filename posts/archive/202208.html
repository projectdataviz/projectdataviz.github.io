How to Participate

Are you a seasoned Data Visualization pro? Then create your viz, publish it to Tableau Public and promote it on social media with the hashtag #ProjectDataViz! Also be sure to submit your viz with us so we can help get the word out about your awesome skills!

If you're not a pro, and want to step into this a bit slower, still create your viz and publish to Tableau Public. Then come back here and submit your viz and ask for feedback. Remember, the #ProjectDataViz team have real jobs too, but we'll do our best to review your viz and provide feedback in a timely manner. Be sure to let the #DataFam know you're growing your Data Viz skills by sharing on social media with the hashtag #ProjectDataViz! There's no shame in learning and developing your skills!
